Theodore's Lair is a new Diablo 1 community where you can meet new players and trade your items with others and play on our B.Net server to play with less lag.

Theodore's lair is also a mod which aims to make the game harder and improve Theo's quest (which can be activated in the settings).

Although this expansion is designed for Diablo: Hellfire, it CAN'T be used in Diablo 1.

Diablo Hellfire - Theodore's lair

- Makes the game harder

- Improves Theo's quest (Hellfire) and makes it much more important.

- The game in Hellfire mode reads .sv files and not .hsv files

(to be able to play with an old character from HF change the name from .hsv to .sv)

DevilutionX is a port of Diablo and Hellfire that strives to make it simple to run the game while providing engine improvements, bugfixes, and some optional quality of life features.

A mod is an unofficial extension. here is a good selection of mods for Diablo 1 and Diablo 1: Hellfire

DevilutionX hope to provide a good starting point for mods, in addition to the full Devilution source code they also provide modding tools.

see the previous section named Welcome to Theodore's lair to download our mod : D1TL (diablo 1 Theodore's lair)
